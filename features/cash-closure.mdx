The Cash Closure screen (Cierre de Caja) is your end-of-day financial view. It aggregates all sales and purchases recorded today into a clear income/expense breakdown and calculates your net profit and average ticket.

## What the screen shows

Four metric cards at the top summarize today's activity:

| Card | What it shows |
|---|---|
| **Total Ventas** | Sum of all completed sale totals for today |
| **Total Compras** | Sum of all purchase costs recorded today |
| **Ganancia Neta** | Net profit: Total Ventas − Total Compras, with margin percentage |
| **Transacciones** | Count of individual sale transactions completed today |

Below the cards, the **Resumen Detallado** section breaks down the same numbers in a structured list and adds two additional metrics:

- **Ticket Promedio** — Average sale value (Total Ventas ÷ number of transactions)
- **Margen de Ganancia** — Net profit as a percentage of total sales revenue

## How to perform a cash closure

<Steps>
  <Step title="Review the day's summary">
    Open **Cierre de Caja** from the Dashboard or the side menu. Check the four metric cards and the detailed summary to confirm the numbers look correct.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Generate the report">
    Tap **Generar Reporte**. The app creates a CSV file with all the day's figures and opens the Android share sheet so you can send it via email, WhatsApp, or any other app on your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reset the day">
    Once you have saved or shared the report, tap **Reiniciar Día** to clear today's sales, purchases, and inventory movements from the active records. A progress indicator is shown while the reset runs.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Generate and save the report **before** tapping Reiniciar Día. The reset deletes today's sales, purchases, and movements and cannot be undone.
</Note>

## Generated report format

The exported file is a CSV named `reporte_cierre.csv` with two columns:

```
Concepto,Valor
Total Ventas,<value>
Total Compras,<value>
Ganancia Neta,<value>
Cantidad de Ventas,<value>
Ticket Promedio,<value>
```

The file is shared through the Android system share sheet and can be opened in any spreadsheet application.

## Movement tracking

In addition to sales and purchases, the app records `MovimientoInventario` events whenever stock changes. Today's movements are available in the ViewModel as `movementsToday` and are included in the reset operation.

### Movement data model

```kotlin
@Entity(
    tableName = "movimiento_inventario",
    indices = [Index("producto_id")],
    foreignKeys = [
        ForeignKey(
            entity = Product::class,
            parentColumns = ["id"],
            childColumns = ["producto_id"],
            onDelete = ForeignKey.CASCADE
        )
    ]
)
data class MovimientoInventario(
    @PrimaryKey(autoGenerate = true) val id: Long = 0,
    @ColumnInfo(name = "producto_id") val productoId: Int,
    val fecha: Long = System.currentTimeMillis(),
    val tipo: String,    // "AJUSTE" | "COMPRA" | "VENTA"
    val cantidad: Int
)
```

Each movement record stores the product it affects, a timestamp, a type (`AJUSTE`, `COMPRA`, or `VENTA`), and the quantity changed. Deleting a product cascades to its movement history.

## CashClosureViewModel state

The `CashClosureViewModel` derives all values directly from live database queries:

| State | Type | Calculation |
|---|---|---|
| `salesTotal` | `StateFlow<Double>` | Sum of today's `venta.total` rows |
| `salesCount` | `StateFlow<Int>` | Count of today's `venta` rows |
| `purchasesTotal` | `StateFlow<Double>` | Sum of today's `compra.total` rows |
| `netIncome` | `StateFlow<Double>` | `salesTotal − purchasesTotal` |
| `avgTicket` | `StateFlow<Double>` | `salesTotal ÷ salesCount` (0 if no sales) |
| `movementsToday` | `StateFlow<List<MovimientoInventario>>` | All inventory movements recorded today |

All flows use `SharingStarted.WhileSubscribed(5_000)` so they stay active while the screen is visible and stop 5 seconds after the screen is left.

<Tip>
Close your day at a consistent time each evening so that "today" comparisons in the Dashboard and Cash Closure always reflect a clean 24-hour window.
</Tip>
